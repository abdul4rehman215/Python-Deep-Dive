4. **Why are decorators useful?**

5. **What is the role of the wrapper function?**  
   It adds extra behavior before or after the original function executes.

6. **Why use `*args` and `**kwargs` in decorators?**  
   To support functions with any number of arguments.

7. **Does a decorator change the original function code?**  
   No, it wraps the function without modifying its implementation.

8. **Where are decorators commonly used?**  
   Logging, authentication, caching, validation, and performance monitoring.
